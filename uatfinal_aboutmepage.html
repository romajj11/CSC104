About me

Hey there, I'm J.J.! Art has been my lifelong companion, expressing my creativity since as far back as I can remember. Recently, I've ventured into the realm of digital art, honing my skills with tools like Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. Yet, amidst the wonders of technology, I still enjoy the authenticity of traditional mediums. Sketching and inking by hand gives me so much more expression, reminding me of the timeless charm of pen on paper. Join me as I navigate the ever-evolving landscape of art. Welcome to my world of endless possibilities and boundless imagination.
